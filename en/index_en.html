Frequently Asked Questions

Didn't get all your answers? Contact us by email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How does it work?

You pay for membership, get a confirmation email, and we arrange a short in-person meeting. You receive your access chip there.

Total Privacy: Autonomous 24/7 access

Train without reception and without waiting. Entry is self-service day and night.

Your Safety: Continuous HD monitoring

Only active members can enter. Shared areas are continuously monitored with HD cameras.

Will it be crowded?

Member capacity is limited, so you have enough space to train comfortably.

Are there cameras in changing rooms and showers?

No. Changing rooms and showers are camera-free.

Can I rent a locker?

Yes! A lockable locker costs 200 Kč per month. Rent a locker here.
