Frequently Asked Questions

Didn't get all your answers? Contact us by email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How does it work?
After payment, you will receive a confirmation email. A staff member will then call the provided phone number to arrange an appointment. At the meeting, you will receive a chip to access the gym.
Where is the staff?
Olympia Gym operates without on-site staff. Trainers are present during the day, but the facility is otherwise unattended.
Who watches my belongings?
Entry is granted only with a valid membership. In addition, the facility is monitored by cameras, and recordings will be provided to the police if needed.
Will no one bother me?
The number of members is limited to ensure a comfortable environment.
Are there cameras in the changing rooms and showers?
No, but all other areas are monitored.
Can I rent a locker?
Yes, we offer personal locker rentals. For a monthly fee of 200 CZK you can rent a lockable locker and don't have to carry your things each visit. Lockers are secure and accessible only during your gym visits. To rent, use the button in the additional services section above or send us an email.
