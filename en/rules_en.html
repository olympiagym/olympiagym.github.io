Rules

These rules govern the mutual contractual conditions that regulate the legal relationships between the operator and its visitors when providing services by the operator to these visitors.

OlympiaGym may only be used by persons over 18 years of age (younger persons only with the consent of a legal representative, but not for persons under 15 years of age), who are pre-registered with the operator, who have been issued a personal entry code ensuring entry to the OlympiaGym premises, and who have thoroughly familiarized themselves with these rules.

Entry to the OlympiaGym premises is prohibited without a properly paid membership. Entry to OlympiaGym is self-service through a chip, which is assigned individually and monitored systematically.

Every member of OlympiaGym is obliged to familiarize themselves with these rules and act and behave in accordance with them.

By paying the membership fee according to the valid price list and/or entering the gym premises, they express full agreement. Members are entitled to use individual equipment and tools of OlympiaGym or use its services based on a properly paid membership according to the established price list. Each member is fully responsible for their health condition.

OlympiaGym is not responsible for any damage to health or property caused by a member intentionally, negligently, or by overestimating their physical condition.

Every member is obliged to wear sports clothing, shoes, and also a towel when exercising on fitness equipment. When performing the respective sports activity, they must not endanger their health or the health of other members and must not damage property.

It is forbidden to pollute the OlympiaGym premises. All outerwear must be stored in the designated place, i.e., in the lockers, or on the bench at the entrance, or left in their bag. Similarly, shoes must be stored in the shoe racks at the entrance to OlympiaGym. OlympiaGym is not responsible for damage to items brought by a member into the gym premises. In case of any damage to equipment (dumbbells, machines), or exercise aids, it is the duty of each member to immediately report it to the operator of OlympiaGym.

The use of such equipment or facilities is strictly prohibited until the defects are removed. Members of OlympiaGym are obliged to behave disciplined and with due caution so as not to endanger their health or the health of others. Otherwise, the operator has the right to terminate the membership of such an individual without compensation. It is forbidden to keep the keys to the lockers when leaving the OlympiaGym premises. After finishing the exercise, everyone must clean up the equipment and tools in their designated place.

There is a strict ban on smoking, consuming alcoholic beverages, narcotics, and other psychotropic substances in the OlympiaGym premises. In all areas except the relaxation zone, it is forbidden to consume food, sweet, and protein drinks.

In exceptional cases and with the prior consent of OlympiaGym, children may move around in the gym, but only accompanied by parents, who explicitly and non-transferably bear full responsibility for any damage to their child's health and the operator's property caused by the child's movement.

Members of OlympiaGym are responsible for damage or loss caused to the operator in full, even if it is an unintentional act. Each member is obliged to empty the locker when leaving the locker room. They are obliged to take care of the locker key and leave the key in the lock of the locker when leaving.

Members are responsible for damage caused to the operator by the loss of the key, damage to the lock, or other misuse. In case of loss, damage, or misuse, the visitor is obliged to pay the amount of 1000 CZK for damage to the lock, 250 CZK for the loss of the key.

Before leaving OlympiaGym, members are obliged to turn off the lights, close the windows, and ensure that the entrance door is properly closed. OlympiaGym informs its members, and members hereby acknowledge and explicitly agree that they will be monitored by a camera system in the OlympiaGym premises. The purpose of operating such a camera system is not only to protect the rights and legitimate interests of OlympiaGym, in the form of general protection of OlympiaGym property, but also to protect the health and property of members moving in the OlympiaGym premises. The camera system records only public areas, it is not operated in areas such as toilets and locker rooms. Only authorized persons have access to the recordings. Camera recordings are deleted every 31 days.
